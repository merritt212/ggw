About Girl Guess What

Girl Guess What is a faith based women's organization geared to enlighten, empower, and unite women from all walks of life. We are a social media network dedicated to women's life stories. We represent women that have experienced God's power or yearn for it. We Stand for a new era of women that are trusting God on their journey to fulfill their God given purpose.

Mission

To showcase and raise awareness of God operating throughout the world in many women's lives. Girl Guess What offers a network of prayer and mentorship that inspires, uplifts, and connects women globally. We, at GGW, provide a platform for a diverse group of women who share about life through testimonies that impact their beliefs, faith, health, culture, daily challenges, experiences, relationships, goals, purpose, success, and interests creating a virtual sisterhood.

Vision

Girl Guess What envisions that by showcasing life stories we are able to bring each other closer to God. We hope this gives women an opportunity to voice themselves in a intimate setting where they can achieve personal fulfillment and growth. As a sisterhood we are motivated to join together to receive healing, clarity, hope, peace, deliverance, love, and favor. We pray women will fellowship and encounter God now!

Marriam Chafera McMillian is an actress, designer, and philanthropist. Born and raised in New York and Dallas, McMillian attended Texas Southern University majoring in business management, and California Design College majoring in fashion design. The founding of "Girl Guess What" stemmed from a testimony of her own, the power to overcome many obstacles, and eventually rising to the beginning of something new and refreshing in her life. As a woman of God, McMillian believes one of her many callings will materialize through acting and "Girl Guess What", an organization that will touch and empower the lives of woman of all ages.
